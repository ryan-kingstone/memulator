Privacy Policy

This is the privacy policy document for Memulator 9000.

  • We will not store data about you for longer than we need to.
  • Files you upload are deleted after 5 minutes.
  • We do save a log of files uploaded, the size, the origin IP address, filename and whether it failed or not for the purposes of improving the service. Unless you specifically write us a message using the data mentioned above, we can't identify you directly.
  • We do not share your data with (the NSA), although we may give out parts of the previously mentioned log to Law Enforcement agencies if we receive some form of legal document that requires us to do so.
